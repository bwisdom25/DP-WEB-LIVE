About Right Stuff Equipment

Right Stuff Equipment represents the Dr. Pharm USA name as the exclusive North American representative for Dr. Pharm. All Dr. Pharm equipment and parts are provided and serviced through Right Stuff Equipment. Founded in 2005, Right Stuff Equipment made its start by selling and servicing used equipment for the food and beverage industry. Soon, Right Stuff began to emerge as a leader in supplying equipment, providing technical expertise, and manufacturing custom built systems. In 2012 Right Stuff Equipment partnered with Dr. Pharm on a new business sector into the pharmaceutical and dietary supplement industry. Right Stuff Equipment became the sole provider and trusted service center for all Dr. Pharm equipment in the North American market. Beyond the partnership with Dr. Pharm, Right Stuff Equipment takes pride in providing engineering solutions for integrated automation equipment. Right Stuff also provides new equipment, automation line layout design, integration logistics expertise, installation services, and equipment training for the food, beverage, supplement and pharmaceutical industries.

Located in beautiful Denver, Colorado, the Right Stuff headquarters facility contains a highly skilled staff of engineers, fabricators, and sales team. Within the 21,000 ft2 facility, Right Stuff also utilizes their in house fabrication and machine shop complete with multiple welding centers, lathes, milling machines, a vertical CNC center, and an in-house water jet Right Stuff is always on the horizon with new opportunities as the company is now building full product lines of automation equipment including: accumulation tables, unscrambling tables, hugger belts, bucket elevators, vertical form and fill baggers, and tray forming machines.
