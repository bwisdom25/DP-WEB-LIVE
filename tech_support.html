Technical Support from Dr. Pharm USA

Right Stuff Engineers are factory trained for all Dr. Pharm equipment and ready to provide you with immediate technical answers to all Dr. Pharm equipment. Engineers are sent to Dr. Pharm headquarters biannually to be trained on all pharmaceutical and supplement equipment Dr. Pharm offers. The in-depth fully hands-on training seminars provide engineers the knowledge and experience they need to service and provide technical support for all Dr. Pharm equipment. Beyond being well versed in the technical aspects of the machines, Right Stuff Engineers strive to understand powder properties and solid dose processes to further provide you with a complete solution. By attending industry seminars and training sessions, our engineers keep up with current technology and trends in the industry.
