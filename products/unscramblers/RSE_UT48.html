RSE-UT48 - Automatic Bottle Unscrambler

The RSE-UT48 is a great entry-level turntable style bottle unscrambler for those with a variety of bottle types. The simple design unscrambles a wide variety of containers with different shape and size. With a 48" diameter tabletop, there is plenty of room for bottle accumulation and results in greater efficiency in the overall packaging process. The RSE-UT48 fits and integrates seamlessly in almost any configuration. The RSE-UT48 bottle unscrambler is best suited for start up companies seeking to start an automated packaging process with multiple container sizes. Contact us Today.
