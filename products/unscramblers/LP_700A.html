LP 700A - Automatic Round Bottle Unscrambler

The Dr. Pharm LP 700A is a great mid level automatic bottle unscrambler for round bottles. The simple design enables easy change over for different sized round pharmaceutical and supplement bottles. The unit is PLC controlled incorporating sensors to automatically start and stop bottle unscrambling. The loading bin provides easy access to load the unscrambler with round bottles. The LP 700A bottle unscrambler is best suited for start up to mid level companies with round style bottles seeking to improve automated packaging process.Download a LP 700A Brochure, or Contact us Today.
