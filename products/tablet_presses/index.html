Automatic Rotary Tablet Press Machines

Dr. Pharm offers a wide range of rotary tablet pressing equipment. Every model is equipped with a pre-compression and main-press roller, both capable of multiple tons of force resulting in more pressure during powder compaction and ultimately better tablet quality. All Dr. Pharm's rotary tablet presses feature automatic weight control enabling full automatic tablet pressing with precise weight control. If a specific tablet shape is required, whether it is round, irregular, embossed, de-bossed, or annular, Dr. Pharm offers quailty tablet press tooling for both TSM and EU standards. Get more information by checking out one of the many tablet presses Dr. Pharm offers, Download a Rotary Tablet Press Brochure, or contact us today.

GZP/ZPT - 265

The GZP/ZPT 265 rotary tablet press is an excellent entry level model. The small footprint machine utilizes both pre-compression and main compression stations to obtain quality tablets. Most suited for start up companies and beginners in automatic tablet pressing

GZP/ZPT - 370

The GZP/ZPT 370 tablet press machine is an excellent entry level model with added output capabilities. The tablet press still retains a small footprint, but the added turret size increases the overall output. Most suited for start up companies and those beginning rotary tablet pressing technology.

GZPD - 560

The GZPD 560 rotary tablet press is the next step model with special features. Three compression stations apply added pressure creating tablets from powders that normally don't form under direction compression. Best suited for small to mid level pharmaceutical companies.

GZPS - 720

The GZPS 720 tablet press machine is a large output model capable of manufacturing single or dual-layer tablets. The rotary uses dual output chutes for single-tablets or using a single discharge chute for dual-layered tablets. This tablet press machine is suitable for companies with large volume demands or those looking to manufacture dual-layer tablets.

GZPT - 1060

The GZPT 1060 is Dr. Pharm's most versatile and largest model. The triple discharge of single layered tablets makes this model the largest output rotary tablet press. Beyond large throughput, the GZPT 1060 is also proficient in producing dual-layer and triple layer tablets. This tablet press is appropriate for large companies that require large volume of tablets.
