GZPT 1060 - Automatic Rotary Tablet Press

The Dr. Pharm GZPT 1060 Automatic Rotary Tablet Press is an advanced level model designed for established companies seeking efficient automation in tablet manufacturing. The GZPT 1060 offers capabilities in triple layer tablet pressing. Each of the press rollers is able to press 11 tons of force. With a total center turret diameter of 1060 mm, the large output model contains added room for D, B, BB or HLS14 sized tooling. Precision adjustment is key to consistent tablet manufacturing, that why each of the press rollers are controlled with a servo driven motor. The GZPT 1060 is a great model for those seeking improvement in advance tablet manufacturing. Download a GZPT 1060 Brochure, or contact us today.
