GZP/ZPT 265 - Automatic Rotary Tablet Press

The Dr. Pharm GZP/ZPT 265 Automatic Rotary Tablet Press is an entry level model designed for starting companies seeking automation in tablet manufacturing. The "GZP" model name refers to contact parts made out of stainless steel for food grade type applications and "ZPT" refers to carbon steel contact parts more suited for industrial applications. With a total center turret diameter of 265 mm, there is plenty of room for D, B or BB sized tooling. Both models feature a force powder feeding mechanism which ensures that every die is filled with powder consistently. This entry level tablet press also features full automation through automatic weight control within the PLC programming. The GZP/ZPT 265 is a great model for those looking to make their start in automatic tablet manufacturing. Download a GZP/ZPT Brochure, or contact us today.
