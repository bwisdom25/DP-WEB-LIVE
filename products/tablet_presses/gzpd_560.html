GZPD 560 - Automatic Rotary Tablet Press

The Dr. Pharm GZPD 560 Automatic Rotary Tablet Press is an intermediate to advanced level model designed for established companies seeking efficient automation in tablet manufacturing. The GZPD offers triple press roller technology that enables some product powders to be directly compressible without having to pre-process the powder material. Each of the press rollers are able to press 11 tons of force each, amplifying the dwell time each tablet is put under pressure. With a total center turret diameter of 560 mm, there is plenty of added room for D, B, BB or HLS14 sized tooling. Precision adjustment is key to consistent tablet manufacturing, that why each of the press rollers are controlled with a servo driven motor. The GZPD 560 is a great model for those seeking improvement in automatic tablet manufacturing. Download a GZPD 560 Brochure, or contact us today.
