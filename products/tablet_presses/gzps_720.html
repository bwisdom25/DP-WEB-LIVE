GZPS 720 - Automatic Rotary Tablet Press

The Dr. Pharm GZPS 720 Automatic Rotary Tablet Press is an intermediate to advanced level model designed for established companies seeking efficient automation in tablet manufacturing. The GZPS offers either dual out feed for single layer tablets or single out feed for bi-layered tablets. There are two sets of pre-pressure and main pressure rollers located on opposite ends (4 Rollers Total). Each of the rollers are able to press 11 tons of force each. With a total center turret diameter of 720 mm, there is plenty of added room for D, B, BB or HLS14 sized tooling. Precision adjustment is key to consistent tablet manufacturing, that why each of the press rollers are controlled with a servo driven motor. The GZPS 720 is a great model for those seeking improvement in automatic tablet manufacturing. Download a GZPS 720 Brochure, or contact us today.
