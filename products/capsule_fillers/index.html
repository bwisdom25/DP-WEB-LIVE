Automatic Capsule Fillers

Explore the variety of Dr. Pharm automatic capsule filler equipment. Whether you are looking to automating your capsule filling process or increase your production capability, Dr. Pharm has an encapsulation machine for your needs. Capsule filler models can output finished product from 400 capsules per minute up to 15,000 per minute. Each filler automatically takes empty capsules and fills each capsule with a set dosage of powder, pellets or granules. Every capsule filler model utilizes a tamping style system with multiple tamping stations that allows encapsulation at a much higher rate compared to other styles. The large diameter dosing discs take advantage of rotational physical forces allowing precision weight control at high speeds. Pocket 400

The Pocket 400 capsule filler is an excellent entry level model. This automatic model is a small footprint machine that still applies the same mechanisms as the large scale models. Most suited for start up companies, beginners in automatic capsule filling, and research and develop purposes.

Pocket 800

The Pocket 800 capsule filler is an excellent entry level model with added output capabilities. This automatic model is a small footprint machine that still applies the same mechanisms as the large scale models. Most suited for start up companies, beginners in automatic capsule filling, and research and develop purposes.

CFM 1200

The CFM 1200 capsule filler is an entry model for versatile product types. The increased footprint machine applies robust cam driven mechanisms to deliver extra power when tamping complex products. Best suited for starter companies with multiple product types.

CFM 2000

The CFM 2000 capsule filler is an excellent mid-level model. The increased footprint machine applies robust cam driven mechanisms to deliver extra power when tamping complex products. The increased output machine is well suited for mid-level companies with multiple product types

CFM 3500

The CFM 3500 capsule filler is a high output model. The large footprint machine applies a powerful cam drive system to distribute strong tamping force to compact complex products. The increased output machine is well suited for mid-level companies with multiple product types with a need for increased production output.

CFM 7500

The CFM 7500 capsule filler is an excellent large output machine. The large scale machine utilizes design features that collectively create high output of finished capsules. The large output machine is well suited for high-level companies requiring large production output with multiple product types.

CFM 15000

The CFM 15000 capsule filler is the largest capsule filling machine in the world. This unit is able to produce capsules at a rate of 15,000 per minute. This machine is most appropriate for large companies that require an extensive amount of product output.
