Pocket 400 - Automatic Capsule Filler

The Dr. Pharm Pocket 400 is an entry level model for companies pursuing full capsule filling automation. This tamping style capsule filler utilizes the same cam drive mechanism as the larger machines but with a smaller footprint. All capsule fillers are able to fill capsule sizes 00 all the way down to 5. The Pocket models also feature an easy to use touch screen HMI allowing operators to change speed parameters, and track overall output. For those looking to start automatic capsule filling or a lower output machine for research, the Pocket 400 is the solution. Download a Pocket 400 Brochure, or contact us today.
