CFM 15000 - Automatic Capsule Filler

The CFM 15000 Automatic Capsule Filler is a largest output model from Dr. Pharm. This model is for large companies pursuing automatic capsule filling on a massive level. This tamping style capsule filler sits on a large stable framework and utilizes a large heavy duty cam drive mechanism. The CFM 15000 is capable of producing 15000 capsules per minute making it the largest capsule filler in the world. All capsule fillers are able to fill capsule sizes 00 all the way down to 5. The CFM 15000 capsule filler models features an independent control cabinet with a large HMI screen allowing operators to change speed parameters, and track overall output. For those looking to start automatic capsule filling with a variety of product types on a very large scale, the CFM 15000 is the solution. Feel free to contact us today.
